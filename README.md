
Smart Bharat – AI Powered Civic Companion 🇮🇳🤖

An AI-powered digital governance platform that transforms how citizens interact with Government services through personalized assistance, multilingual support, intelligent scheme recommendations, and smart grievance management.

🌟 Overview

Smart Bharat is a next-generation AI-powered civic platform inspired by the Digital India vision. It acts as an intelligent digital companion that helps citizens discover government services, access welfare schemes, report public issues, manage important documents, and receive personalized assistance—all from a single platform.

Unlike traditional government portals, Smart Bharat uses Artificial Intelligence to simplify complex procedures, recommend relevant services, explain government documents in simple language, and provide multilingual voice-enabled assistance.

🚀 Problem Statement

Government services are spread across multiple websites, making it difficult for citizens to:

Find the correct government service
Understand eligibility criteria
Apply for schemes
Track complaints
Manage documents
Understand government notifications
Access services in their preferred language

Smart Bharat solves these challenges through an AI-powered unified governance platform.
